### 表"不可能"的假设
#### 1. 表示和<font color="#92d050">现在</font>事实相反
If I <span style="background:#d3f8b6">saw</span> the rabbit now, I <span style="background:#d3f8b6">would</span> bite him.
![[image-155.png]]

#### 2. 表示和<font color="#92d050">将来</font>事实相反(可能性很小)
If I <span style="background:#d3f8b6">should see</span> the rabbit, I <span style="background:#d3f8b6">would</span> bite him.
![[image-156.png]]

![[image-157.png]]

#### 3. 表示和<font color="#92d050">过去</font>事实相反
If I <span style="background:#d3f8b6">had seen</span> the rabbit a few days ago, I <span style="background:#d3f8b6">would have bitten</span> him.
![[image-158.png]]
