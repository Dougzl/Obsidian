### 2. 过去将来进行时
(他说明年这个时候他还会爱着我)   He said that at this time next year, he would still be loving me.

### 3. 过去将来完成时 
(他承诺，到我们金婚时，他已经爱了我50年)  He promised that by our golden anniversary, he would have loved me for 50 years.

### 4. 过去将来完成进行时 
(他说，到明年我们就结婚10年了，他会一直爱着我)  He said that by next year, we would have been married for 10 years, and he would have been loving me all that time.
