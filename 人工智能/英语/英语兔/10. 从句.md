### 从句核心原理 [原片 @ 01:45](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=105)
#### 基本定义
- **从句(Subordinate Clause)**：充当句子成分的句子（不能独立存在）
- **主句(Main Clause)**：从句依附的主体
- **复杂句(Complex Sentence)**：主句+从句的组合 *![](http://localhost:8000/static/screenshots/screenshot_1d83d36fe-b9d0-4352-8749-53def720dd5f.jpg)

#### 形成机制
- **例句演变**：
  - 简单句："The rabbit ate a carrot"（主+谓+宾）
  - 复杂句："I saw [that the rabbit ate a carrot]"（[ ]内为宾语从句）[原片 @ 01:56](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=116)
- **本质**：句子降级为句子成分（主语/宾语/定语等）

### 从句分类体系 [原片 @ 04:44](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=284)
#### 按句子成分分类
| 从句类型       | 功能                 | 示例                          |
|----------------|----------------------|-----------------------------|
| 主语从句       | 充当主语             | **That he came** surprised us |
| 宾语从句       | 充当宾语             | I know **what you did**      |
| 表语从句       | 充当表语             | The truth is **that we lost**|
| 定语从句       | 充当定语             | The book **which you lent**  |
| 状语从句       | 充当状语             | He left **when it rained**   |
| 同位语从句     | 解释抽象名词         | The fact **that Earth is round** |

#### 按词性分类
- **名词性从句**：主语/宾语/表语/同位语从句（相当于"大名词"）
- **形容词性从句**：定语从句（相当于"大形容词"）
- **副词性从句**：状语从句（相当于"大副词"）*![](http://localhost:8000/static/screenshots/screenshot_2ba73cf1a-1d44-48b9-8245-cce88a9b214a.jpg)

### 形容词性从句（定语从句）[原片 @ 07:31](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=451)
#### 核心思维差异
- **中文模式**：前置修饰（"刚从地里回来的乌龟那儿买来的胡萝卜"）
- **英文模式**：核心名词+关系词+补充说明
  `The rabbit ate a carrot [that I bought]` *![](http://localhost:8000/static/screenshots/screenshot_33e8f346d-21f0-42a2-b7c6-6927291f725f.jpg)

#### 关系词系统
| 类型         | 功能                     | 词例                     |
|--------------|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| 关系代词     | 替代先行词在从句中作成分 | who/whom/which/that/whose |
| 关系副词     | 在从句中作状语           | where/when/why          |
| **特殊规则**：
  - 唯一性先行词（the only/first）必须用 **that**
    `the only rabbit [that ate carrots]` [原片 @ 18:51](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=1131)

#### 限定性 vs 非限定性
| 类型         | 功能               | 标点     | 示例                          |
|--------------|--------------------|----------|-----------------------------|
| 限定性       | 缩小范围（必需）   | 无逗号   | Rabbits [that eat carrots] are cute |
| 非限定性     | 补充说明（可删）   | 有逗号   | This rabbit, [which ate carrots], is full *![](http://localhost:8000/static/screenshots/screenshot_40027af1c-a433-45ff-be94-c126e9d10646.jpg) |
| **特殊用法**：
  非限定性从句可修饰整个句子
  `He passed the exam, [which amazed everyone]` [原片 @ 23:48](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=1428)

### 名词性从句 [原片 @ 24:24](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=1464)
#### 主语从句
- **基本结构**：
  `[That the rabbit is hungry] is obvious`
  （主语从句+谓语）*![](http://localhost:8000/static/screenshots/screenshot_52b0d45ba-2c85-4ac3-b627-65f58e679843.jpg)
- **形式主语(it)**：避免头重脚轻
  `It is obvious [that the rabbit is hungry]` [原片 @ 29:11](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=1751)
- **虚拟语气触发词**：
  `It is essential [that he **be** here]`（be动词原形）[原片 @ 31:33](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=1893)

#### 宾语从句
- **时态呼应规则**：
  主句过去时 → 从句用相应过去时态
  `I knew [he **liked** carrots]`（非客观事实）[原片 @ 36:41](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=2201)
- **否定转移**：
  `I **don't** think [he is right]`（而非 I think he isn't right）[原片 @ 35:43](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=2143)
- **省略规则**：
  连词that可省略（除并列从句等特殊情况）`I hope (that) you win`

#### 表语从句
- **位置**：系动词后
  `The question is [whether we should go]` *![](http://localhost:8000/static/screenshots/screenshot_67b6cddcc-47b4-46b7-96ba-8e22e9b545de.jpg)
- **常见系动词**：
  be/seem/appear/feel
  `It seems [that he lied]`

#### 同位语从句
- **修饰对象**：抽象名词（fact/idea/question等）
  `The news [that we won] excited us` *![](http://localhost:8000/static/screenshots/screenshot_7c5d82beb-6514-487a-aa51-340563fcc549.jpg)
- **vs 定语从句**：
  同位语解释名词内容（=），定语从句修饰性质（≈形容词）

### 副词性从句（状语从句）[原片 @ 49:52](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=2992)
#### 九大类型及引导词
| 类型       | 引导词                     | 示例                          |
|------------|----------------------------|-----------------------------|
| 时间       | when/while/as/before/after | He left [before I **arrived**] |
| 地点       | where/wherever             | Stay [where you **are**]     |
| 条件       | if/unless/provided         | [If it **rains**], we cancel |
| 让步       | though/even if/no matter   | [Though tired], he continued |
| 方式       | as/as if                   | Do [as I **say**]           |
| 比较       | than/as...as               | She's taller [than I **am**] |
| 原因       | because/since/as           | [Since you **ask**], I'll tell |
| 目的       | so that/in order that      | Study hard [so that you **may pass**] |
| 结果       | so...that/such...that      | It's [so] hot [that I **melt**] |

#### 重点规则
1. **时间从句时态**：
   用一般现在时代替将来时
   `I'll call you [when I **arrive**]`（非 will arrive）*![](http://localhost:8000/static/screenshots/screenshot_8d8045d74-b1df-45eb-bd7f-75f2b8dc314d.jpg)
2. **条件从句虚拟**：
   与事实相反 → 用过去式表虚拟
   `[If I **were** you], I'd go` *![](http://localhost:8000/static/screenshots/screenshot_9a1a58758-cc10-4a28-b346-b7b2f24d282b.jpg)
3. **让步从句禁忌**：
   避免 though/but 连用（逻辑冲突）
   `[Though tired], he worked`（√）
   `Though tired, **but** he worked`（×）[原片 @ 07:27](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1XY411J7aG_p11?t=447)
